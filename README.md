## Features

- **Add Tasks**: Enter your tasks and assign them a priority (Low, Normal, High). Because let’s face it, some tasks are just more important than others, like "Watch cat videos" vs. "Finish that report."
  
- **Task Management**: Complete or delete tasks with just a click! Say goodbye to the days of scribbling out tasks on paper. Now you can feel that sweet, sweet satisfaction of clicking "Complete" without the risk of ink stains.

- **Useless Mode**: Toggle "Useless Mode" to receive absurd tasks and humorous notifications. Because who doesn't want to be distracted by the existential dread of counting clouds or organizing their sock drawer by emotion?

- **Filtering**: View all tasks, only active tasks, or completed tasks. This way, you can avoid facing the reality of how many tasks you’ve actually completed (spoiler: it’s probably not many).

- **Local Storage**: Your tasks are saved in the browser's local storage. So even if you close the tab in a moment of panic, your tasks will be waiting for you like a loyal puppy.

- **Responsive Design**: Works well on both desktop and mobile devices. Perfect for when you need to procrastinate on the go!

## Usage
- Enter a task in the input field. Try to keep it realistic (or absurdly unrealistic, we don’t judge).
- Select the priority level from the dropdown. Remember, “Low Priority” is just code for “I’ll do this when I feel like it.”
- Click the "Add Task" button to add it to your list. Feel the rush of accomplishment as your list grows... or the dread as you realize how much you have to do.
- Use the buttons to mark tasks as complete or delete them. Just don’t delete your responsibilities; they have a way of coming back (like that annoying relative).
- Toggle "Useless Mode" to enjoy humorous notifications and additional absurd tasks. Warning: side effects may include uncontrollable laughter and existential musings.
